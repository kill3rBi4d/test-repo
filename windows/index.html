Play high quality music in your Discord server for free.

CK Music is one of the best discord music bots that you can find and provides free features which are payed in other bots!!

Already being used by most discord servers.




Get started in 3 steps

1. Invite the bot to your server

Start by inviting the bot onto your discord server using this link.


2. Play a song

Once CK Music has joined your server, join a voice channel and do $ckplay or $ckp [ music/url ]. You can replace music by the music that you want to play or a spotify album/playlist link.


3. Enjoy the music!

Close your eyes and listen!



Command List

To use a command, do $ck[command_name] Replace command_name with the name of the command. Example: $ckhelp . If you need any help, check out the Starter guide


Commands Alias Description Usage
play [$ckp] Automatically fetches the video(s) and joins the voice channel you are in to play your query! $ckplay [name/URL]
clear-queue [$ckcq] Clears the current queue. $ckclear-queue
filter [$ckf] Filters the playlist. &ckfilter [filter name]
loop [$cklp] [$ckrepeat] Loops the current playing music. &ckloop
nowplaying [$cknp] Lists the current playing music(s). $cknowplaying
pause [$ckps] [$ckfreeze] Pauses the playing music. $ckpause
queue [$ckq] [$ckline] Adds a music to the queue. $ckqueue
resume [$ckrs] [$ckrewind] Resumes the current playing music(s). $ckresume
search [$cksr] Searches and lists all results from the query. $cksearch [name/URL]
shuffle [$cksh] Shuffles the current playlist. $ckshuffle
skip [$cksk] [$ckjump] Skips the current playing music. $ckskip
stop [$ckdc] Stops the playing music. $ckstop
volume [$ckvl]< amount > [$ckrise-to]< amount > Used for increasing and decreasing the volume. $ckvolume< amount >
w-filters [$ckfilters] List of all filters enabled or disabled. $ckw-filters
